Dharamshala

Embark on a journey to the enchanting hills of Dharamshala, nestled in the serene embrace of the mighty Himalayas. Be captivated by the breathtaking landscapes, from lush green meadows to snow-capped peaks. Dharamshala, the abode of the Dalai Lama, offers a spiritual sanctuary with Tibetan monasteries and prayer flags fluttering in the mountain breeze.

Ooty

Embark on a serene retreat to Ooty, a picturesque hill station cradled in the lap of the Nilgiri Hills. Breathe in the crisp mountain air as you wander through lush tea plantations and meandering pathways adorned with vibrant flowers. Ooty, often referred to as the 'Queen of Hill Stations,' charms with its colonial architecture, featuring charming cottages and gardens.

Goa

Discover the vibrant charm of Goa, where golden beaches meet azure waters and lively nightlife. Embrace the rich cultural tapestry, adorned with colorful festivals, Portuguese-influenced architecture, and delectable seafood.

From the tranquil beaches of Palolem to the bustling markets of Mapusa, Goa invites you to unwind, explore, and indulge in a tapestry of sun, sand, and celebration
